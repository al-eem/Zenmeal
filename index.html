OUR PURPOSE

Born From Necessity, Driven By Hope

Since the start of the war, daily life for millions in Ukraine has been unimaginably altered. The simple act of preparing a hot, nutritious meal has become a luxury many cannot afford. Families have been displaced, living in shelters without kitchens, stable electricity, or even clean water. In these moments, food is more than sustenance—it is survival, energy, and hope.

Traditional food aid, while vital, is often bulky, difficult to transport, and may not provide the balanced nutrition needed to endure long-term stress. We saw a critical gap: the need for a smart, practical, and dignified nutritional solution that works in the toughest conditions. That is why we created ZenMeal.

Introducing ZenMeal

A complete, ready-to-use meal designed for life on the move, in shelters, or on the frontlines. It’s real food, in a smarter form.

●

Nutritionally Complete

Each serving provides 400-500 calories with a perfect balance of protein, healthy fats, fiber, and over 25 essential vitamins and minerals.

●

Crisis-Ready

With a shelf-life of up to 12 months, our meals require no cooking, refrigeration, or special preparation. Just open and consume.

●

Locally Inspired

We use familiar Ukrainian flavors like honey-oats, vanilla buckwheat, and berry blends to provide comfort when it's needed most.

●

Adaptable Formats

Available as a ready-to-drink bottle for immediate use or as a lightweight powder sachet to be mixed with water.

Who We Help & How

Our mission is to support every part of our community. By choosing ZenMeal, you are not just buying a product; you are contributing to a nationwide effort of recovery and resilience.

Displaced Families & Individuals

We provide a reliable source of nutrition for those in shelters or temporary housing, ensuring children and adults receive the vital nutrients they need without a kitchen.

Soldiers & Frontline Workers

Our lightweight, high-energy meals are perfect for soldiers, medics, and volunteers who need portable nutrition to stay strong and focused during long hours.

Supporting the Ukrainian Economy

We are proudly "Made in Ukraine." By sourcing our ingredients like oats and sunflower oil from local farms, we support Ukrainian agriculture and create jobs for displaced workers.
